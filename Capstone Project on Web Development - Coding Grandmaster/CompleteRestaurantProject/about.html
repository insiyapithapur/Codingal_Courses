Our Story

Sam's Food Corner started as a small family restaurant. Our goal was to serve fresh, affordable and delicious meals to everyone.

Today, we serve a wide variety of Indian, Chinese and Italian dishes. Our chefs carefully prepare every meal using fresh ingredients.

We believe that food brings people together. That is why we work hard to give every customer a comfortable and enjoyable dining experience.
